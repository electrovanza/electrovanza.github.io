Electrovanza

A technical event organized by the ECE & EEE department ofMAHE Dubai.

ABOUT

Electrovanza is a technical event organized by the ECE & EEE departments of School of Engineering & IT in Technovanza. Are you wise enough to escape from the prison of Alcatraz? Does your mind work amazingly fast even in difficult situations? Do you think you have a good presence of mind? Then come & crack the way to escape from the Alcatraz of Electrovanza '18. An event filled with lots of mind boggling Electronic & logical puzzles!
WHEN: May 2nd-3rd, 2018
WHERE: B Block, 2nd Floor, MAHE Dubai

LEVEL 1 - ESCAPE ROOM

RULES & REGULATIONS

1. You have 20 minutes to escape from the room. All clues needed to solve the puzzles are in the room.
2. Each team must contain 4 to 5 members.
3. Solve all the puzzles by using critical thinking and not by physical power.
4. Do not move anything with a red dot sticker. Items marked with a red dot.
5. You are able to ask for help 3 times during the game to have your time officially count. More hints will be given, however, your time will not count.
6. The rooms are equipped with security cameras to monitor safety and track progress.
7. Please exit the same unlocked door in case of an emergency.
8. Please keep the clues, solutions for the puzzles & combinations to yourself so others can enjoy the game after you.
9. We kindly ask you to take care of the rooms & the objects found in there. If anything is damaged, you may be asked to leave the room & pay for the damages.
10. The team to escape the room using the least time wins.

LEVEL 2 - SMART CAR & IoT BASED APPLICATIONS

RULES & REGULATIONS

1.Team size allowed is 4-5 members.
2.1 Laptop per team is mandatory for the participants.
3.All the required components for this stage will be provided.
